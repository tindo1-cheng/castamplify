Privacy Policy

Last updated: 3 July 2026

CastAmplify ("we", "us") operates castamplify.com, a platform for podcast and audio creators. This policy explains what personal information we collect, why we collect it, and the choices you have. We aim to keep it in plain language.

1. Information we collect

  • Account details — your name, email address, and chosen creator niche, provided when you sign up.
  • Profile details — your bio, profile photo, and niche tags, if you choose to add them.
  • Content details — episode titles, descriptions, and links you register on the platform, and links to your social or podcast profiles.
  • Payout email — if you provide one in preparation for creator payouts.
  • Technical basics — we store your setup progress in your own browser (localStorage) so the platform remembers you between visits. We do not run advertising trackers.

2. How we use your information

  • To create and operate your CastAmplify account and dashboard.
  • To generate AI-assisted content for you: when you register an episode, its title and description are sent to our AI provider (Anthropic) to produce show notes, captions, and title suggestions for you.
  • To send you service emails, such as a welcome email when you sign up, via our email provider (Twilio SendGrid).
  • To understand, in aggregate, how the platform is used so we can improve it.

3. Where your information lives

Your account and profile data is stored in our database hosted by Supabase. Our website and functions are hosted by Vercel. Emails are delivered by Twilio SendGrid. Episode text you submit for AI generation is processed by Anthropic. Each of these providers processes data on our behalf under their own security and privacy commitments.

4. What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not send you marketing email you haven't signed up for.
  • We do not display third-party advertising or advertising trackers on the platform.

5. Your rights

Depending on where you live (including under UK and EU GDPR), you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or receive a copy of your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. To exercise any of these rights, email us at hello@castamplify.com and we will respond as soon as we can.

6. Data retention

We keep your account data while your account is active. If you ask us to delete your account, we will remove your personal data from our systems within 30 days, except where we are legally required to keep it longer.

7. Children

CastAmplify is not directed at children under 16, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from them. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, contact us and we will delete it.

8. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy as the platform grows. We will post the updated version on this page with a new "last updated" date, and for significant changes we will notify you by email.

9. Contact

Questions about privacy? Email hello@castamplify.com.
