How it works

Three stages. Two gates. One certificate that means something.

Every course runs on a published clock — two weeks per module, hard deadlines, one paid re-sit. Your punctuality, marks, and effort build your SkillBridge Score, which employers use to pick placement candidates. Being on the ball is the curriculum.

STAGE 1 · WEEKS 1–12

Theory, on the clock

Six two-week modules: lessons, practice question banks, and an AI tutor inside every module for whenever you're stuck. Quizzes marked instantly; written tasks marked by AI against a published rubric.

Gate: pass every module at 60%+, on time.
STAGE 2 · WEEKS 13–32

Search, then work

Build your Showcase Profile, keep a weekly Placement Search Record with evidence, and secure a placement through our Placement Board — at a wage you and the employer agree upfront.

Gate: no placement, no progression. Effort is tracked.
STAGE 3 · WEEKS 33–40

Advance and certify

Advanced modules plus a capstone built from your own placement. Pass it and your certificate is issued, your profile turns Graduate, and you join the Graduate Jobs Board.

Outcome: certificate + lifetime jobs board access.

Poultry: Broiler Production

Day-old chick to market bird in seven weeks — and you, from beginner to certified broiler producer with real farm experience behind you.

WKS 7–8B4 — Feeding & Growth: feed phases, FCR, weighing to target weight
WKS 9–10B5 — Health, Vaccination & Biosecurity: Gumboro, Newcastle, farm biosecurity
WKS 11–12B6 — Slaughter, Marketing & Records: costing, pricing, the full-cycle budget
